Popular Books in Marathi Lanuage

  • Yayati

    • Author:V.S.Khandekar

    Yayati is a very popular book in the Marathi language. It is based on king yayati and other characters from Mahabharta, this novel is regarded as one of the best literary masterpieces ever written.


  • Mrutyunjay

    • Author:Shivaji Sawant

    Suryputra Karn a character from Mahabharata, who spends his whole life being mocked as Sutputra this novel is based on his life.


  • Kosla

    • Author:Bhalchandra Nemade

    One boy name Pandurang Sangvikar came to Pune from a village in Khandesh. He is the main character of this novel.this novel revolves around him.


  • Panipat

    • Author:Vishwas Patil

    This book is based on the history of the Maratha and the Afghan War. It's called as the third war of Panipat.panipat is a place where this battle happens.


  • Fakira

    • Author:Anna Bhau Sathe

    The novel is based on a rebel named Fakira who inspires social justice and freedom. In 1961, the novel received the Best Novel award from the Maharashtra government.


  • Sambhaji

    • Author:Vishwas Patil

    This novel is based on the life of Chhatrapati Sambhaji Maharaj eldest son of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj.
