Author:Tukaram Bhaurao Sathe

Born:1 August 1920

Place:Wategaon village,Sangli,Maharashtra.

Tukaram Bhaurao Sathe, also known as Anna Bhau Sathe, was a social reformer, writer, and folk poet from Maharashtra. His notable works include the novel "Fakira" (1959), which received a state government award in 1961.(Source wikipedia)
