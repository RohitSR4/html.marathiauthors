Author:Vishnu Sakharam Khandekar

Born:11 January 1898

Place:Sangli,Maharashtra.

Khandekar embarked on his writing journey in 1919 with the publication of his debut work, "Shrimat Kalipuranam." His prolific career extended until 1974 with the release of his novel "Yayati." In 1920, Khandekar began his role as a schoolteacher in the town of Shiroda, located in the contemporary Sindhudurg district of Maharashtra's Konkan region.(source wikipedia)
