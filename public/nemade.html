Author:Bhalchandra Nemade

Born:27 May 1938

Place:Sangvi,Yaval,Maharashtra.

Bhalchandra Nemade, born on 27 May 1938 in Sangavi village in Maharashtra's Khandesh region, had an extensive academic journey. After completing his matriculation, he relocated to Pune. He earned his BA from Fergusson College and MA in Linguistics from Deccan College, both in Pune. (source wikipedia)
